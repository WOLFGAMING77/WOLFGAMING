FAQ

1. How long does delivery take?

Digital items are usually delivered within 5-15 minutes after payment verification. Please note that first-time orders may require a standard security check by our payment gateways, which can occasionally extend the fulfillment time.

2. Is it safe?

Yes. We use industry-standard encrypted protocols and partner with trusted global payment gateways (like Transak, Banxa, and Simplex) to ensure your data and transactions are 100% secure.

3. What if I entered the wrong ID or Wallet?

Contact our support immediately via Telegram or Email with your Order ID and the correct information. Please be aware that once a transaction is processed on the blockchain, it cannot be reversed.

4. What is your Refund Policy?

As per our Terms of Service, all successful digital deliveries are final and non-refundable due to the nature of cryptocurrency. However, if a delivery fails to be fulfilled by our system, a full refund will be issued to your original payment method within 24 hours. We maintain a 'Digital Delivery Proof' log for every order to ensure transparency.
